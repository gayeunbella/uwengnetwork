## Inspiration
UW engineering students are isolated across departments, co-op terms, and social circles. They work on projects solo with no easy way to find teammates and waste hours on Discord, Instagram, and websites for opportunities such as research or projects, only to face cold email rejections. Meanwhile, professors struggle to discover skilled, interested students that can help with their research.

We would like to bridge this gap by bringing together like-minded people who would like to collaborate and contribute to projects or research, providing a platform where we can find people on campus to work with or network with. 

## What it does
UW Network connects Waterloo engineering students and professors, where they can:
-  Post and share the projects or ideas they're working on
- Easier research opportunities to find on campus, where students and professors can find them and reach out to them.
- Find your community and collaborate on interesting, cool projects or research!
- Send messages to people; no need for email, just do it here!
- Searchable by department and research interest, scraped from public UW faculty pages.
- **Discrete Likes**The author sees who noticed them, nobody else does.
- **WATCARD Verification** ONLY for UW Engineering students, verified via WATCARD photo upload.
